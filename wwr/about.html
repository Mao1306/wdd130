Dry Oar

At Dry Oar, our purpose is to connect people with the natural world through exhilarating rafting experiences. We believe in the transformative power of outdoor adventures, fostering a deep appreciation for our planet's rivers and landscapes.

Our mission is to be the leading provider of safe, memorable, and environmentally responsible rafting experiences. We exceed customer expectations by delivering exceptional service, expert guidance, and unparalleled access to some of the world's most breathtaking rivers. Through sustainability and community engagement, we inspire a lifelong love for outdoor exploration and conservation.

History

Dry Oar was founded in 1995 by a group of passionate outdoor enthusiasts who shared a deep love for adventure and nature. Starting from humble beginnings with just a few rafts and a vision to share their love for rafting with others, Dry Oar has grown into a reputable name in the rafting industry. Over the years, we've expanded our operations to offer a variety of rafting trips, from thrilling whitewater adventures to serene river explorations, all while maintaining a strong commitment to safety, sustainability, and exceptional customer experiences.
